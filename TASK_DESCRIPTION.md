# [799928. My.Games] US Conversion Analysis

## Funnel Breakdown by Top Payment Methods (Bank Card, PayPal, Google Pay)

- The US accounts for 64.6% of total payment traffic with an average conversion rate of 80.68%.

The partner requested a detailed US conversion analysis.

## Task

Prepare a detailed conversion breakdown for the US market across top payment methods (Google Pay, PayPal, Bank Card):

1. Payment funnel per method.
2. Top error codes per method.
3. Cancellation reasons by type (Suspected Fraud, Insufficient Funds, Generic Declines, etc.).

## Analysis Period

June-December 2025 and January up to the latest available date in March.
